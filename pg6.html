Wintermute

Page 6: Leveling Off 

 
      
  As you level off a strong gust catches the plane. Had you dumped the fuel the plane would have been pushed into the trees 
  skimming by just feet from the wingtips as you decended into the valley. 

  Now that you are below the peak of the valley, the wind is noticably reduced.

  The frozen river grows closer.

Dump the Fuel

Dump the remaining fuel in the small plane. with the engines dead, it's just deadweight at this point this landing is going to be tough enough without any fireballs.

Engage the Flaps

You feel the plane dropping too quickly, deploying the flaps might give you the lift you need to make it to the frozen river
