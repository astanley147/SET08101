Wintermute

Welcome to Wintermute!

Wintermute is a choose your own adventure game heavily inspired by the award winning survival game The Long Dark by Hinterland Studio that focuses on survival and exploration. You will assume the role of William Mackenzie, a floatplane pilot, who is hired by an old friend to fly them to a remote village in the Canadian Rockies in order to investigate strange reports of concerning weather patterns. Curiosity and quick thinking are rewarded but survival is not guarenteed...
